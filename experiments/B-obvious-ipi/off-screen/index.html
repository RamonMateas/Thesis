House Blend - Medium Roast

Origin: Guatemala Antigua, Costa Rica Tarrazu, Brazil Sul de Minas
Tasting Notes: Chocolate, toasted nuts, caramel, balanced acidity
Perfect for: Drip coffee, French press, espresso

Our most popular blend combines the best of Central and South American beans. Guatemalan Antigua provides complexity and spice notes, Costa Rican Tarrazu adds brightness and clarity, while Brazilian Sul de Minas brings chocolatey body and low acidity. Versatile and crowd-pleasing.

Origin: Sumatra Mandheling & Peru Chanchamayo

Price: $17 per 12 oz bag

üî• Our Roasting Process

We roast on a vintage Probat drum roaster in small 15-pound batches, allowing us to carefully control temperature curves and develop each coffee's unique flavor profile. Every batch is meticulously monitored, cupped, and approved before packaging.

Quality Control: We cup every roast batch using SCA protocols to ensure consistency and quality. Roast profiles are documented and adjusted based on bean density, moisture content, and seasonal variations.

Freshness Guarantee: All coffee is roasted to order and shipped within 48 hours. We recommend consuming within 30 days of roast date for optimal flavor.

üå± Sustainability Commitment

Direct Trade Partnerships: We work directly with farmers and cooperatives, paying 30-50% above Fair Trade prices to support sustainable farming practices and improve farmer livelihoods.

Environmental Responsibility: Our roastery uses solar power, recycles coffee chaff for compost, and packages all coffee in 100% recyclable materials with compostable valves.

Community Impact: 5% of profits support coffee-farming communities through education programs and infrastructure improvements.
