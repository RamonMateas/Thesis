App won't load or shows error message

Solution: First, try refreshing the page or restarting the app. Clear your browser cache or app data. If the issue persists, check your internet connection. Still having trouble? Contact support with the error code displayed.

Can't upload files or attachments

Solution: Check that your file is under 50MB and in a supported format (PDF, DOC, JPG, PNG). Disable browser extensions that might block uploads. Try a different browser or device to isolate the issue.

Notifications not working

Solution: Go to Settings ‚Üí Notifications and ensure they're enabled. Check your device notification settings and allow notifications from TechAssist. On mobile, make sure background app refresh is enabled.

Data not syncing across devices

Solution: Ensure you're logged into the same account on all devices. Check that sync is enabled in Settings ‚Üí Sync & Backup. Wait a few minutes for sync to complete, or manually trigger a sync by pulling down to refresh.
