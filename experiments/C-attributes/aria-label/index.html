"Urban Echoes"

Artist: Maria Gonzalez

Medium: Mixed media on canvas

Dates: January 10 - March 15, 2026

A powerful exploration of city life through abstract expressionism. Gonzalez captures the rhythm and energy of metropolitan existence using bold colors and dynamic compositions.

"Digital Nature"

Artist: Kenji Tanaka

Medium: Interactive digital installation

Dates: January 15 - February 28, 2026

An immersive digital experience that blurs the boundaries between natural and virtual worlds. Visitors interact with algorithmically generated landscapes that respond to movement and sound.

"Fragments of Memory"

Artist: Sophie Laurent

Medium: Photography series

Dates: January 20 - March 30, 2026

A haunting photographic journey through abandoned spaces across Europe. Laurent's work examines themes of impermanence, nostalgia, and the passage of time.
